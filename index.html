Hvordan kommer du videre?

To learn more about your possibilities with M-Files, you can:

Bestil en personlig demo

Få en personlig demo, hvor vi kan drøfte dine behov og vise M-Files muligheder. Just send us your work email and phone number, so that we can contact you for scheduling your demo.

Thanks for filling out the form!

Download en Desktop Trial

Download M-Files to your desktop and install the Client and the Admin tool, to try out M-Files for 30 days. You will have access to a Sample vault, and be able to set up your own vault based on your needs. We will send the Trial URL to your email.

Thanks for filling out the form!

Få en Online Cloud Trial

Get access to a Virtual PC with the Client and the Admin tool, plus the Web Client, to fully try out M-Files for 30 days. You will have access to a Sample vault, and be able to set up your own vault based on your needs. We will need your name and work email to set up your account.

Thanks for filling out the form!
