Hvordan vil det foregå, hvis du beslutter at implementere M-Files?

As Nordic M-Files Gold Partners, we are certain that you will find the principles behind M-Files helpful in your search for the right system for you. We can start your Evaluation period today by implementing the Free M-Files Trial for you (download it to your desktop or set it up in the Cloud).

If you decide to go ahead with M-Files, the implementation is divided into 3 phases:

1. Professionel kortlægning af behov

Typically, we will begin discussing the scope of your document management project already during your M-Files evaluation period.

As the scope and possibilities in M-Files become clearer, we can move on to plan our involvement in the implementation and post-launch phases.

Some projects require more help from us, others much less.

2. Hurtig og professionel implementering

Do It Yourself
We can readily deliver a standard solution and help you with the initial setup, while teaching your administrator to master M-Files for your company requirements.

Hjælp til det du har behov for
We will gladly assist in the implementation at any capacity you wish, such as tailoring your data structure; migrating or indexing your existing data, set up your workflows, and more.

Fuld professionel assistance
Solution Management og M-Files understøtter det hele.

Extended functionality
M-Files is also an easy development platform, so in the event that no standard solution covers all your needs out-of-the-box, we can add additional functions quite cost-effectively.

3. Efter lancering

- Vi hjælper dig som du vil have det
- Videre udvikling af funktioner
- Fuld maintenance og support fra M-Files
